Rajzyngier Research

Rajzyngier Research is a private legal research lab. It studies legal infrastructure — identity, consent, trust, and the physical systems those depend on — with a working preference for research that lands as apparatus rather than pure theory.

Two questions organise the lab's work.

01What is justice?

02What would it take to change society from one where parents can disown their kids into one where kids can disown their parents?

The lab's current working project is Box Fraise — a hair salon and the software that runs it. Every part of Box Fraise is the lab's attempt to answer those two questions in physical form: the salon, the events, the Legal Justice Fund, the portrait program, the software.
