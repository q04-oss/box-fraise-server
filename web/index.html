For businesses

American Apparel used their own employees and customers in every ad for a decade. No models, no celebrities. It worked because the people were real.

We do a version of that. The person in your ad is a Box Fraise client who agreed to it — not your employee, not a model, not a stock photo. They share in the fee. Because they don't work for you, nobody can accuse the ad of being staged with your own staff, which turns out to matter.

We take a cut. The rest goes to them.

Businesses that keep licensing portraits usually get pulled further in — coming to events, sitting a consultation, eventually running their own scheduling and appointments on the software Box Fraise runs. The portraits are how you meet us.
