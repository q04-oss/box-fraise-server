The method

Charcoal roasting has a two-hundred-year tradition in Japan and almost no tradition in Canada. We're bringing it here one batch at a time — a slower, more attentive way of turning green coffee into what you drink.

Every bag is roasted the week you order it. We ship on Tuesdays.
